## 🧠 Why Dice 21?

Dice 21 blends classic Blackjack strategy with dynamic dice mechanics to create a simple, addictive risk-reward experience.

- 🎲 Roll your way toward 21 — but don’t bust  
- ⚖️ Risk increases as your total climbs  
- 📈 Unlock higher stakes and bigger payouts  
- 🏆 Progress through tiers, achievements, and tournaments  

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Place your bet  
2. Roll the dice  
3. Choose to **Hit** (roll again) or **Stand**  
4. Beat the dealer without going over **21**

💡 **Under 14:** roll **2 dice**  
💡 **14 or higher:** roll **1 die** (higher risk)
